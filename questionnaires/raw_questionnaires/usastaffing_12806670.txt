Select the one statement that best describes the Minimum Requirements that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a WG-2610-12 Electronic Integrated Systems Mechanic.

2.
Select the one statement that best describes the Specialized Experience and/or education that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a WG-2610-12 Electronic Integrated Systems Mechanic.
 I HAVE at least 24 months experience or training 1) in utilizing principles, basic functions and application of theory of electronic integrated systems equipment, 2) in diagnosing problems and determining needed repairs for malfunctions in electronic integrated systems equipment, 3) in making mechanical repairs that required the use of precision measuring and calibrating equipment, 4) in using a wide variety of test, measurement, and diagnostic equipment (TMDE), and 5) which demonstrates my ability to interpret technical manuals, specifications, and publications.
 I DO NOT have at least 18 months of the experience, education or training listed above.
