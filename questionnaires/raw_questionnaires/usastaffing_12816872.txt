From the descriptions below, select the appropriate response based on your level of experience, education, and/or training, that you fully possess and that demonstrates your ability to perform Industrial Worker duties without more than normal supervision.

 I have the ability to perform the most difficult and complex task of an Industrial Worker without more than normal supervision. I have the ability to physically handle, move, segregate cleaning, unpack, pack, mark, label and palletize a variety of munitions or explosives. Assemble primers, tracers, fuzes and propellant bags to munition. And extract munitions from containers and sort the different metals. I am considered an expert and work with a high degree of independence.
 I have the ability to perform the full range of an Industrial Worker. I am skilled in performing manual tasks involving explosives and explosives related items in a manufacturing, production or warehouse environment and assists in the demilitarization of ammunition. I work on my own initiative under general supervision subject to occasional inspection.
 I have the ability to perform common tasks of Industrial Worker's duties in a production environment assembling items, handling materials, unloading boxes and placing the material at the correct production phase, replacing defective products, sorting items utilizing physical characteristics and packaging finished product and utilizing manual assembly and automated production equipment. I work under normal supervision of a work leader or journey-level employee who observes tasks in progress and upon completion to make sure they are properly performed.
 I have the ability to perform simple tasks of Industrial Worker's duties. I have received training in a manufacturing, production or warehouse environment.
