1.
From the descriptions below, select the appropriate response based on your level of experience, education, and/or training, that you fully possess and that demonstrates your ability to perform DRILL RIG OPERATOR duties without more than normal supervision.
 I have the ability to perform the most difficult and complex tasks and provide advanced assistance in operating various drill rigs, utilizing a wide range of sophisticated drilling techniques. I am skilled in operating power auger drill rigs designed for shallow-depth drilling to explore various geologic areas; preparing for drilling jobs by aligning the rig and installing proper bits and devices; operating control levers of a drill rig under normal drilling conditions; drilling in various types of formations, such as clay, sand or silt; performing field maintenance of drill rig equipment such as the drive shaft, cylindrical buckets, related bits, pumps, and motors. I am considered an expert and work with a high degree of independence.
 I have the ability to perform the full range of drilling techniques on mechanically operated drill equipment. I am skilled in assisting in core drilling operations; assists in the setting up and operation of drilling equipment to obtain samples of soil, rock, etc., for field classification and/or laboratory analysis; assisting in setting up equipment such as leveling machine and setting up mast on drill rig; assembling and disassembling drill rods using wrenches and other tools; assisting in removing samples from sample barrels; assisting in maintenance of drilling equipment by cleaning tools and equipment and oiling and greasing parts. I work on my own initiative under general supervision subject to occasional inspection.
 I have the ability to perform some tasks including assisting with the common drilling techniques on mechanically driven drill rigs. I can assist with common drilling techniques on mechanically driven rigs and support journeyman workers in the use of drill bits, other drilling equipment. I support the performance of drilling techniques and procedures to ensure proper site modification prior to drilling, and aid in the storage, proper care and condition of equipment and materials for the job. I have the ability to repair, operate, maintain, and perform routine repairs of drill rig equipment. I work under supervision of a work leader or journey-level employee who reviews my work for completion.
 I have the ability to perform common drill rig operation tasks. I am skilled in simple drilling techniques under supervision. I have assisted as a helper in using drilling equipment. I have the ability to repair, operate, maintain, and perform routine repairs of a wide variety of equipment. I have spent time working outside in a variety of weather conditions. I can provide safe assistance to field operations, follow directions to prepare site prior, and load proper equipment and materials for assignments. I can troubleshoot, repair, operate, and maintain small equipment and engines. I can drive a manual transmission vehicle. I am considered an expert and work with a high degree of independence. I work under close supervision of a work leader or journey-level employee who observes tasks in progress and upon completion to make sure they are properly performed.
 I have the ability to perform simple tasks related to drilling functions or I have received training as a helper worker in using assisting with drilling equipment. I have the ability to repair, operate, maintain, and perform routine repairs of a wide variety of equipment.
