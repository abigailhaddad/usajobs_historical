1. Comprehensive knowledge to review position descriptions and develop performance standards. Ability to develop, direct, and evaluate the work and performance of subordinate supervisors and employees.
2. Comprehensive knowledge of principles, concepts, and practices involving the ARNG Surface Maintenance facilities, manpower, equipment, and services. Ability to use current automation to monitor and analyze if established ratios for direct and indirect labor are maintained, competencies for achieving production goals are assessed, and workflow in the facilities attain established efficiency and backlog goals.
3. Comprehensive knowledge to in the oversight of the quality assurance program for assigned surface maintenance facilities to ensure production goals as established are attained. Ability to monitor maintenance activities for utilization, turn-around-time, funds expenditures, stock management, and manpower utilization to meet those goals.
4. Ability to plan and execute ongoing surface maintenance facility visits throughout the state to evaluate efficient operations, provide assistance with workflow operations, resolve problems with supported units, and promote compliance in accordance with current regulations and policies.
5. Ability to conduct periodic inspections of maintenance activities to determine adequacy of work, storage; and to support current density and type of equipment supported and personnel employed. Ensures the State Maintenance Support Plan (SMSP) is reviewed and updated to reflect the most current data and inspections are recorded accurately for Installation Status Reporting - Infrastructure (ISR-I).
6. Comprehensive knowledge to manage the safety and occupational health program for the maintenance facilities and operations including instruction in safety operating procedures, comprehension of governing policies, investigation of all accidents/incidents and determining corrective action to ensure procedures are implemented to prevent future problems.
7. Ability and knowledge to analyze the needs, develop requirements, and coordinate recommendations to ensure surface maintenance facilities possess, maintain, and properly account for expendable, non-expendable, and durable property. Knowledge of full scope of requisition process and ability to review flow of materials and supplies required to support maintenance activities effectively.
8. Able to communicate effectively to develop performance standards, advise and counsel subordinate supervisors or staff, make or resolve decisions on work problems, and interview candidates for appointment, promotion or reassignment.
