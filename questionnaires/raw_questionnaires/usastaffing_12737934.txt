Assessment 1
Once the application process is complete, a review of your resume and supporting documentation will be completed. If, after reviewing your resume and supporting documentation, it is determined that you have misrepresented your qualifications and/or experience, a revised assessment will be rendered.
1. Select the one statement that best describes the Minimum Requirements that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a WG-3809-09 Mobile Equipment Metal Worker.
 A. I HAVE experience or training which has provided me with 1) a knowledge of mobile body construction and 2) the skill to remove, fabricate, reshape and replace or repair such damage as, dents, tears, wrinkles, cuts and creases by cutting, knocking out, welding, filling and sanding.
 B. I DO NOT HAVE the experience or training listed above.
2. Select the one statement that best describes the Specialized Experience and/or education that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a WG-3809-09 Mobile Equipment Metal Worker.
 A. I HAVE at least 18 months experience or training 1) which has provided me with the knowledge of mobile equipment construction and how all components and parts are installed and determine the total extent of damage and repair alternatives, 2) in the use of gas, arc, and welding equipment, 3) in the use of trade equipment, 4) in applying body filler to build up depressed areas, and the skill to smooth by grinding and sanding, 5) in applying primers paint with brush or spray gun, gravity feed and HVLP (high volume low pressure) equipment, and 6) in mathematical calculations and scribe patterns in the fabrication of wood or metal parts having straight lines or irregular curves.
 B. I DO NOT have 18 months of the experience, education or training listed above.
