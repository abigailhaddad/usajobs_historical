Thank you for your interest in the Blocker and Bracer Leader position with the Department of the Army. We will evaluate your resume and your responses to this assessment questionnaire to determine if you are among the best qualified for this position. Your responses are subject to verification.


1.
From the descriptions below, select the appropriate response based on your level of experience, education, and/or training, that you fully possess and that demonstrates your ability to perform Blocker and Bracer duties without more than normal supervision.

 I have the ability to perform the most difficult and complex as a working leader of a number of employees in accomplishing the proper positioning of containers in boxcars, gondolas, flatcars, and truck trailers. I am skilled in providing technical guidance on the proper methods of blocking and bracing equipment such as artillery, ammunition items or general supply items using blocks, braces and other devices. I have lead others who haul high explosives, munitions and their components over highways operating semi- trucks with tractor- trailers with air brakes; evenly distributing weight of cargo to ensure safe transport. I have instructed others on the methods of assembling, installing and repairing nonstandard wooden articles used in storing, renovating and transporting ammunition. I am considered an expert and work with a high degree of independence.
 I have the ability to perform the full range of a working leader on a temporary basis or as part of my journeyman level job description on how to properly block and brace general equipment using blocks, braces and other staying devices. I have provided guidance to employees operating gasoline or diesel-powered truck tractor with semi-trailers using hydraulically powered controls to haul regular and/or hazardous materials. I am skilled in instructing others involved with loading and unloading trucks and moving and storing munitions in a warehouse using a forklift I work on my own initiative under general supervision subject to occasional inspection.
 I have the ability to perform common duties being a working leader. I can give directions to a number of employees in accomplishing task such as blocking and bracing equipment on truck trailers using blocks, braces, or other staying devices. I have operated truck tractors with semi-trailers and Strad-O-Lift trailers to haul explosives, munitions, and components. I work under close supervision of a work leader or journey-level employee who observes tasks in progress and upon completion to make sure they are properly performed.
 I have the ability to perform simple work leader duties or I have received training on giving directions to a number of employees in accomplishing tasks such associated with the work of a Blocker and Bracer Leader performing basic securing of loads.
