Assessment 1
Once the application process is complete, a review of your resume and supporting documentation will be completed. If, after reviewing your resume and supporting documentation, it is determined that you have misrepresented your qualifications and/or experience, a revised assessment will be rendered.
1. Select the one statement that best describes the Minimum Requirements that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a WG-6904-06 Tools and Parts Attendant.
 A. I HAVE experience or training which demonstrates 1) my knowledge of trade, 2) my ability to apply identification skill (matching specific item name, models, and stock or part numbers with similar specific information at stock locations), and 3) my ability to use simple hand tools (pliers, screwdrivers, etc) and to follow oral and written instructions.
 B. I DO NOT HAVE the experience, education, or training listed above.
2. Select the one statement that best describes the Specialized Experience and/or education that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a WG-6904-06 Tools and Parts Attendant.
 A. I HAVE at least 9 months experience or training 1) in verifying stock by opening, counting and visually checking the contents of the containers, 2) in establishing stock location by considering such factors as use, size, shape and weight of items, 3) in completing stock records by showing the names, quantities and location of stock, 4) in identifying correct nomenclature and stock number of items in order to locate stock for issue, and 5) in performing maintenance such as replacing wooden handles on handsaws, changing blades on power saws, and greasing or oiling power and hand tools.
 B. I DO NOT have at least 9 months of the experience, education or training listed above.
