Assessment 1
1. Please select one response that best describes how you meet the basic education requirement for the GS-0610, Professional Nurse Series.
 A. I have successfully completed a graduate or higher level degree, bachelor’s degree, associate degree, or diploma from an accredited professional nursing educational program accredited by the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education, Council on Accreditation of Nurse Anesthesia Educational Programs, Accreditation Commission for Midwifery Education, or an accrediting body recognized by the U.S. Department of Education at the time my degree was obtained. (Note - Official certification from the Commission on Graduates of Foreign Nursing Schools is required for individuals who graduated from foreign nursing schools). AND I have passed the National Council Licensure Examination. AND I possess a current, active, full, and unrestricted license or registration as a professional nurse from a State, the District of Columbia, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, or a territory of the United States.
 B. I have successfully completed a PhD or equivalent doctoral degree from a professional nursing educational program or related medical science field.
 C. I do not have the required education and licensure as required above.
2. Please select one response that best describes how you meet the minimum qualifications for a GS-0610-11, SUPERVISORY NURSE (RESUSCITATIVE MEDICINE TRAINING EDUCATOR).
 A. I have successfully completed a diploma, associate degree, bachelor’s degree, or master’s degree from a professional nursing program AND I have at least one full year of professional nursing experience equivalent to the next lower grade level (GS-09) or pay band in the federal service or equivalent experience in the private or public sector. - Experience performing nursing functions, principles, and practices to teach critical care courses relating to resuscitative medicine for proper response actions to medical emergencies. - Experience having administrative oversight of training programs for ongoing professional development of nursing and medical staff while conforming to clinical standards and protocol impacting the patient-care outcomes. - Experience utilizing resourcefulness and judgment to interpret, apply and adapting guidelines of resuscitative medicine training in emergent situations. - Experience in conducting research and performing annual reviews of continuous process improvements of resuscitate training programs.
 B. I do not have the experience and/or education described above.
