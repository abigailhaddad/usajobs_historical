1. Specialized Experience is one-year of experience at the GS-09 level, or equivalent, that has equipped the applicant with the particular knowledge, skills, and abilities to perform successfully the duties of this position as listed above. Specialized experience is defined as:

Providing guidance and supervision over an organization that involves the protection of property against such hazards as fire, theft, damage, accident, or trespass;
AND
Maintaining order and protecting life;
AND
Using investigative techniques and ability to write reports.
 A. I have one full year (52 weeks) or more of specialized experience equivalent in responsibility and scope to the GS-09 level as described above.
 B. I do not have one full year of specialized experience equivalent to the definition as described above.
