Experience: In addition to meeting the basic requirements, do you possess one (1) year of specialized experience equivalent to the GS-4 level. Creditable experience includes but is not limited to receiving and validating authorization forms and requests for information; using various computer applications to track and release all authorized ROI requests; processing and releasing health information to authorized first- and third-party requestors; and initiating and maintaining the accounting of disclosure for all records disclosed from a Privacy Act system of records.
