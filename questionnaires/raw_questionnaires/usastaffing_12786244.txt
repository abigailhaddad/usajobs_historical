Assessment 1
Once the application process is complete, a review of your resume and supporting documentation will be completed. If, after reviewing your resume and supporting documentation, it is determined that you have misrepresented your qualifications and/or experience, a revised assessment will be rendered.
1. Select the one statement that best describes the minimum requirements that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a WG-11 Surface Maintenance Mechanic.
 A. I HAVE experience or training which demonstrates my knowledge of equipment to be able to do routine jobs, simple preventative maintenance tasks, and I am able to use common tools and equipment in the line of work.
 B. I DO NOT possess the above experience or training.
2. Select the one statement that best describes the specialized experience and/or education that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a WG-11 Surface Maintenance Mechanic.
 A. I HAVE at least 18 months experience or training diagnosing, repairing, overhauling, and modifying more complex vehicles, equipment, and more complicated systems; experience which has provided a thorough knowledge of diagnostic equipment; experience applying independent judgment in determining methods and techniques required to solve unusually complex maintenance and repair problems; experienced in testing, inspecting, and evaluating the work performed on vehicles, equipment and systems.
 B. I DO NOT possess the experience above.
