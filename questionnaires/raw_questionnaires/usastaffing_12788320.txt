Assessment 1
Thank you for your interest in a Power Support Systems Mechanic position with Department of the Army. We will evaluate your resume and your response to this Occupational Questionnaire to determine if you are among the best qualified for this position. Your responses are subject to verification. Please review your responses for accuracy before you submit this questionnaire.
1. Read the following statements (A-E) and select the one response that best describes your ability to perform the duties of a Power Support Systems Mechanic.
 A. I have independently performed all of the following duties, and I am consulted by other employees or in difficult situations regarding performance of the following duties: troubleshooting, overhauling, rebuilding, and repairing a variety of power support systems and equipment on various heavy equipment systems; diagnosing, troubleshooting, and repairing electrical and transistorized systems in AC and DC applications; testing and repairing gun fire simulators; and servicing and repairing air conditioning and refrigeration units including control circuits.
 B. I have performed the majority of the following duties with minimal supervision: troubleshooting, rebuilding, and repairing a variety of power support systems and equipment; troubleshooting and repairing electrical and transistorized systems and equipment; and servicing and repairing air conditioning and refrigeration units including control circuits.
 C. I have performed the majority of the following duties under normal supervision: troubleshooting and repairing a variety of power support systems and equipment; troubleshooting and repairing electrical systems and equipment; and servicing and repairing air conditioning and refrigeration units.
 D. I have assisted a higher graded worker or performed the following duties under very close supervision: repairing a variety of power support systems and equipment; repairing electrical systems and equipment; and servicing air conditioning or refrigeration units.
 E. My experience is not reflected in the above statements.
