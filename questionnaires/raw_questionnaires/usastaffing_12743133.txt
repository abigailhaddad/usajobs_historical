3. Lead Social Services Assistant Position Qualification Requirement

Select the one statement below that best describes the education and/or experience that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a Lead Social Services Assistant, GS-08:
 A. Specialized Experience: I possess at least one (1) year of specialized experience equivalent to at least the next lower grade level (GS-07) in the normal line of progression for the occupation in the organization. In order to meet the minimum qualifications for this position, you must meet at least 3 of the 4 following specialized experience requirements to minimally qualify: Locating individuals in crisis and identifying emergency services available; Linking emergency services to individuals in crisis and relaying/communicating information to emergency service; Applying rules, policies, and procedures related to crisis hotlines; Applying analytical skills to review and report on crisis line data and trends to recommend improvements.
 B. Applicants may substitute education for the required experience. I possess at least one and a half years (1.5 years) of graduate-level education leading to a Master's Degree or equivalent graduate degree that is directly related to the work of this position that demonstrates my ability to perform the duties of this position. One year of graduate study is usually considered to be 18 semester hours unless otherwise indicated by the school attended. (Transcript required)
