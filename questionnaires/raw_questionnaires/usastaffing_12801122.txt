2. Grade Requirement - GS-08

Experience. 
Must have at least one (1) year of experience comparable to the next lower grade level of GS7 which demonstrates the knowledge, skills, abilities, and other characteristics related to the duties of the position to be filled. This would be experience which provided a knowledge of the more complex procedures as well as knowledge of the uncommon and/or rare sleep disorders.

In addition, candidate must demonstrate the following Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities (KSAs).
Advanced knowledge or the International Classification of Sleep Disorders and the ability to recognize uncommon and/or rare sleep disorders
Ability to summarize and report polysomnographic data in narrative form
Knowledge of event characteristics (e.g., respiratory, cardiac, sleep stage, seizures, etc.) and ability to take appropriate action
Ability to provide briefings and orientation to hospital staff including physicians

Do you meet the experience and KSAs as described above?
