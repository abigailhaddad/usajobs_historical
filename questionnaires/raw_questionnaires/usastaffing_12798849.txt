Specialized experience
- Knowledge of the principles, theoretical concepts and practice of vocational rehabilitation and psychiatric rehabilitation as well as the administrative mechanisms appropriate to the assignment.
- General knowledge and experience in job development, marketing, securing jobs, and the ability to contact and work creatively with community employers.
