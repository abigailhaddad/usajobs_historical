Operates, maintains, and repairs three 500-ton York MaxE Centrifugal Liquid Chillers and one Steam Absorption Chiller during cooling months. These are all accompanied by pumps, piping, water treatment systems, cooling towers, and electronic control systems all requiring preventive and corrective maintenance.
