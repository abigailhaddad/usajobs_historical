 A. Power system operator experience with centralized SCADA/EMS control of power system equipment with high voltage power lines and substations.
 B. Power plant or control center operator experience, where normal duties include direct SCADA control over multiple, remotely operated power plants.
 C. Operations or maintenance experience executing switching programs for clearances or other general switching actions on power system equipment at high voltage levels preferably at or above 34.5kV.
 D. I do not meet any of the requirements as described in A, B, or C above.

 A. Power system operator experience with interconnected system monitoring of voltages, power flows, maintaining limits, responding to changing system conditions, meeting safety, reliability, and coordination requirements, and adhering to applicable policies, procedures, and guidelines; and Applying power system switching procedures to prepare, check and direct routine and emergency switching activities; and Using a Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA)/Energy Management System (EMS) to monitor system conditions and respond to contingencies; and, currently NERC certified.
