 A. I have at least one year of full-time specialized experience or the equivalent, comparable to at least the GS-06 grade level in Federal Service. This experience includes, in addition to the specialized experience required at the GS-06 grade level, activities such as: 1) Use scientific knowledge to determine best sites for data collection; 2) Plan work for field crews, exercising judgment to collect the best data possible without compromising safety; 3) Ensure data is collected according to established protocols, providing guidance and training as necessary; 4) Summarizes and interprets data in reports for use by park managers and staff; 5) Collect scientific field data in a variety of terrains, including aquatic environments (e.g., creeks, rivers, estuaries, lakes).
