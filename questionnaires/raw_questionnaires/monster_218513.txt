The Core Focus of the ECQs are cross-cutting and should be addressed within your two-page resume.&nbsp; The following are the foundation for success in each of the ECQs below: Constitutional fidelity and service to the American people; Efficient resource use, cost-saving, process modernization; Technical excellence and high-quality performance; Team leadership, accountability, performance culture; and Meeting and exceeding mission-aligned goals. ECQ 1 - This core qualification encompasses the ability to uphold the Constitution and American governing ideals - a foundation for federal leadership. ECQ 2 - This core qualification involves the ability to demonstrate effective resource management, budget discipline, waste reduction, and leveraging technology or process improvements. ECQ 3 - This core qualification stresses the delivering of exceptional quality work through technical skill, knowledge, and reliable performance. ECQ 4 - This ECQ involves the ability to inspire and guide teams toward mission objectives while fostering accountability, performance, and adaptability. ECQ 5 - This core qualification involves consistently delivering outcomes aligned with organizational goals and the broader mission set by leadership. MANDATORY TECHNICAL QUALIFICATIONS:&nbsp; All candidates must address the following Mandatory Technical Qualifications within their two-page resume. Demonstrated ability to manage and oversee complex litigation activities, including developing legal strategies, supervising case preparation, and ensuring compliance with applicable laws, regulations, and court rules.&nbsp; Proven experience providing authoritative legal advice on high-impact cases that have significant policy, regulatory, or financial implications. Proven ability to lead and direct the development, interpretation, and application of legal policies related to agency or organization's programs and operations.&nbsp; Experience in anticipating legal challenges, shaping litigation strategy to align with organizational priorities, and advising senior leadership on controversial issues of the highest priority, risk management and policy implications Demonstrated experience managing attorneys and support staff in a litigation environment, including allocating resources, setting priorities, ensuring quality work products, and fostering a collaborative and ethical work culture.&nbsp; Ability to mentor and develop staff, and ensure timely delivery of legal services under tight deadlines.
