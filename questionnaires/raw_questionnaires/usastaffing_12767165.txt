Read all responses before making your selection. If you leave this question blank, you may be rated ineligible at the GS-07 level.

Specialized experience demonstrates the knowledge, skills, and abilities to successfully perform the duties of a Physical Science Technician. Specialized experience is work such as planning and implementing established natural resource surveys and inventories related to one or more physical sciences, utilizing microscope applications and laboratory procedures to assess ecosystem health, and preparing comprehensive project reports that utilize data analysis to define resource status.

For example, 6 months of specialized experience (50% of the specialized experience) and 9 graduate semester hours (50% of the education) would qualify an applicant for this position.
