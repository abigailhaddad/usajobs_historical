2. GS-13 Specialized Experience: Do you possess at least one (1) full year of specialized experience, which equips you with the particular knowledge, skills, and abilities to perform successfully the duties of the Supervisory Engineering Technician, GS-13 position, and that is typically in or related to the work of the position to be filled. To be creditable, this specialized experience must have been equivalent to the GS-12 grade level in Federal service to qualify at the GS-13 grade level.
Examples of specialized experience include:
Experience studying and analyzing work operations as well as making changes in workflow, methods, priorities, etc., to assure uninterrupted services.
Ability to provide comprehensive technical advice and assistance to automation engineering staff on all CMOP automated equipment systems, such as controller, sensors, bar code scanners, personal computers, disk drives, data terminals, local area networks (LANs), pulse and counting mechanisms, video cameras and monitors.
Experience supervising and providing comprehensive technical advice on the rebuilding, overhauling, installing, troubleshooting, repairing, modifying, calibrating, aligning, and maintaining of the CMOP multiple integrated automation systems and all interrelated and interdependent systems included within the overall system.
Ability to prepare and issue written instructions and procedures and special instructions on new developments and automation equipment.
Ability to adherence to current environmental laws and regulations.
