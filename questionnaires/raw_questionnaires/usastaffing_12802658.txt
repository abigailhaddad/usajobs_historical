Electronic System Control Craftworker (TC-K) duties without more than normal supervision.
 A. I have the ability to perform the most difficult and complex duties of the Electronic System Control Craftworker position. I am skilled in performing advanced journey level work in diagnosing, repairing, and maintaining digital processors, encoders, decoders and other solid-state electronic equipment associated with remote control of hydroelectric projects. I am responsible for certifying the electronic equipment meets standards with complete authority for independent determination to keep system in operation or returning it to operation. I am considered an expert and work with a high degree of independence.
 B. I have the ability to perform the full range of duties of the Electronic System Control Craftworker position. I am skilled in the diagnosis, repair, and preventative maintenance for digital processors, teletypewriters, encoders, decoders, and other solid-state electronic equipment on my own initiative, subject to occasional inspection. I am able to receive guidance and assistance from a systems control engineer or other electronic specialist and have the ability to maintain electronic equipment and place into operation. I work on my own initiative under general supervision subject to occasional inspection.
 C. I have the ability to perform common tasks of the Electronic System Control Craftworker position. I have the ability to assemble, install, repair, maintain, and testing real-time digital electronic control computer hardware and software, and digital electronic control equipment. I receive oral instructions as the nature of repairs, installation, or maintenance to be made, and can proceed to carry out the work, receiving advice and assistance as required, in cleaning, making adjustments and/or repairs, calibration, and retest of equipment including various relays, meters, recorders, controllers, indicators, radio transmitters and receivers, oscillators and amplifiers, electronic relays and other electronic and communication equipment. I work under the supervision of a work leader or journey-level employee who reviews my work for completion.
 D. I have the ability to perform simple electrical and electronic duties. I am able to perform simple tasks having minimal procedural steps on work related to electronic or communications equipment. I work under the close supervision of a work leader or journey-level employee who observes tasks in progress and upon completion to make sure they are properly performed.
 E. I do not possess the abilities described above.
