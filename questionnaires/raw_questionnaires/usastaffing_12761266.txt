Assessment 1
Once the application process is complete, a review of your resume and supporting documentation will be completed. If, after reviewing your resume and supporting documentation, it is determined that you have misrepresented your qualifications and/or experience, a revised assessment will be rendered.
1. Select the one statement that best describes the Minimum Requirements that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a WG-10 Artillery Repairer.
 A. I HAVE experience or training which demonstrates 1) the ability to perform mechanical, hydraulic, electrical, and pneumatic system operations, and 2) the ability to fit and adjust parts and assemblies
 B. I DO NOT HAVE the experience or training listed above.
2. Select the one statement that best describes the Specialized Experience and/or education that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a WG-10 Artillery Repairer.
 A. I HAVE at least 18 months experience or training demonstrating 1) a broad knowledge in repairing, rebuilding, modifying and testing mechanical, electrical, and hydraulic systems and major components of artillery systems, 2) in diagnosing malfunctions and determining needed repairs in artillery systems, 3) the ability to inspect artillery systems, 4) the ability to develop test standards, and 5) interpreting and applying drawings, schematics, blueprints, diagrams and specifications.
 B. I DO NOT have at least 18 months of the experience, or training demonstrating listed above.
