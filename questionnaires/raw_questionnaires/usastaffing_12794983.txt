Assessment 1
1. From the description below, select the letter that best describes your level of specialized experience and must meet at least 3 of the 5 specialized experience statements to be fully qualified.
 A. I have one year of specialized experience at the next lower grade (NH-03/GS-13) or equivalent in the federal service (experience may have been gained in the private sector). Specialized Experience is defined as: demonstrating expertise in managing facility services, operations, and sustainment by developing, assessing, and reviewing program costs and schedules, evaluating contractor performance, and approving work orders and project modifications. Providing senior-level guidance on enterprise-wide facilities policies, programs, and space management while effectively navigating organizational dynamics and engaging with senior executives, General Officers, and Flag Officers. Executing service support operations including space planning, warehouse logistics, supply distribution, transportation, official mail management, and janitorial services in alignment with DoD and MDA policies. Delivering effective resource management by balancing funding, staffing, and equipment, implementing internal control and process improvement initiatives, and optimizing life-cycle investments to support mission requirements. Demonstrating full-scope supervisory experience by assigning work, evaluating performance, managing hiring actions, resolving personnel issues, and representing facilities sustainment and modernization efforts to senior stakeholders. Experience must be reflected in your resume.
