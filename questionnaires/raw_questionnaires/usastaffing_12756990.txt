Assessment 1
Select the appropriate answer to the following question based on your ability to perform the duties of this position. When answering, remember that your answers are subject to verification by investigation. You may be asked to provide specific examples or documentation of your knowledge or skills as proof to support your answer, or you may be required to verify your response by a practical demonstration of your claimed abilities.
1. From the descriptions listed below, select the response that best describes your experience as a Boiler Plant Operator, which includes, but is not limited to: Knowledge of the structure and operating characteristics of boilers and associated auxiliary equipment. Knowledge of the location/function of numerous pumps, valves, regulators and gauges. Knowledge of the chemical and physical characteristics of fuels and principles of combustion, steam generation and heat transfer. Knowledge of the relationship between fuel quality and efficient combustion characteristics. Knowledge of water, analysis and basic chemical treatments. Skill in adjusting air temperature, draft and other furnace conditions. Ability to recognize malfunctioning equipment/systems and potentially dangerous operating conditions. Skill in the use of hand tools, electric and pneumatic power tools. Skill in applying preventive maintenance procedures, and limited operational repairs (e.g. cleaning equipment, greasing and oiling moving parts). Ability to read and work from blueprints, drawings, and diagrams to diagnose and locate problem areas for repair. This is a screen-out element. Failure to meet a minimum level will result in an ineligible rating. Select only ONE letter.
 A. I have vocational training or I have completed an apprenticeship.
 B. I have experience performing the duties of a Boiler Plant Operator under close supervision.
 C. I have experience performing the duties of a Boiler Plant Operator under normal supervision.
 D. I have experience performing the duties of a Boiler Plant Operator independently under limited supervision.
 E. I have experience as a Boiler Plant Operator, or I am consulted by other trade workers in difficult situations because of my expertise in this area.
