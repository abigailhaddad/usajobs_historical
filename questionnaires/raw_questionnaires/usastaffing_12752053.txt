Assessment 1
1. I qualify at the GS-08 because:
 A. I have one (1) full year of specialized experience at or equivalent to the GS-07 level that is equivalent in difficulty and complexity as indicated by the following examples: Performing prescribed fire operations including preparation, ignition, monitoring, holding and mop-up. Monitors fire behavior, fire effects, and fire weather on prescribed fires. Collecting fire data such as fuel loading, tree stand density, fuel moisture, maps projects, weather, and maintains records. Operating, maintaining and making minor repairs to chainsaws. Using specialized firing equipment (e.g. fuses, drip torches, flares, and terra torch). Performing wildland fire suppression activities.
 B. I do not have the experience described above.

I have held, or currently hold, the Interagency Fire Program Management (IFPM) qualifications or higher: Engine Boss (ENCG), OR Crew Boss (CRWB), OR Firing Boss (FIRB), OR Helicopter Manager (HMGB), AND Incident Commander Type 4 (ICT4). Failure to provide evidence of my qualifications will result in a rating of "Not Qualified".
