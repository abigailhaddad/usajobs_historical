Specialized Experience is experience using specialized equipment to electronically and/or photographically document the crash environment and vehicle damage; analyzing electronic data obtained from vehicles or external sources; writing factual and analytical investigation reports; exchanging information concerning highway safety with other stakeholders such as other federal agencies, industry, labor, manufacturers, national and professional organizations, associations.
