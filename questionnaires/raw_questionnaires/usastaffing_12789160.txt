1. To qualify at the GS-12 level, you must have one year of specialized experience equivalent to the GS-11 level obtained in the public or private sector, that has equipped you with the necessary knowledge, skills, and abilities to successfully perform the duties of this position. For this position, specialized experience is defined as performing assignments, projects, or studies related to supporting: the Commission's FOIA program. This includes providing administrative management support based on knowledge of an office's mission and functions, goals, and programs; gathering information, identifying and analyzing issues, or participating in activities to improve the effectiveness and efficiency of the office's work operations in support of organizational program objectives.

 B. No
