 A. I have independently made operational decisions and adjustments for a public surface and ground water treatment facilities ensuring compliance with a State or Federal Safe Drinking Water regulatory agency. I have experience, under the direction of senior operators, of making operational decisions and adjustments for a wastewater treatment facility permitted by a State or Federal regulatory agency. I have experience independently performing the following: checking and making operational/plumbing adjustments to system components such as chlorinators, filter banks, sand/debris separators, pumps and controls. I also have experience in taking chlorine residuals; bacteriological water samples; have measured NTUs of water, data calculations, etc. I also have kept accurate records of the maintenance and operations dealing with water and wastewater treatment facilities.
 B. I have independently made operational decisions and adjustments for a public ground water treatment facility ensuring compliance with a State or Federal Safe Drinking Water regulatory agency. I have experience, under the direction of senior operators, performing various maintenance and operational functions (not including operational decisions or operational adjustments) for a wastewater treatment facility permitted by a State or Federal regulatory agency. I have experience independently performing the following: checking and making operational/plumbing adjustments to system components such as chlorinators, pumps and controls. I also have experience in taking chlorine residuals; bacteriological water samples, tabulating data etc. I also have kept accurate records of the maintenance and operations dealing with water treatment facilities.
 C. I have experience, under the direction of senior operators, in operating, maintaining, and repairing public ground water systems (evaluating and making adjustments and repairs as well as checking water treatment devices for proper operation). I have experience in checking and making operational/plumbing adjustments to system components (such as chlorinators, pumps, controls, etc.); taking chlorine residuals; taking bacteriological water samples, etc. I have experience performing minor repairs on water systems. I have experience in accurate keeping of maintenance and operational records dealing with water and facilities.
 D. I have a little experience working at a water and/or wastewater treatment facility.
 E. I do not have experience operating in either the water or waste water field.
