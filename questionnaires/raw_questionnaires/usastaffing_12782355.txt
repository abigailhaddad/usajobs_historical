Assessment 1
Specialized Experience for the GS-15: You must have one year of specialized experience at a level of difficulty and responsibility equivalent to the GS-14 grade level in the Federal service. Specialized experience for this position is defined as proven proficiency and supportable (as evidenced in the resume) hands-on experience in ALL of the following:
Interpreting and drafting legislative, financial, and programmatic guidance; AND
Collecting, analyzing, and integrating detailed and/or ambiguous information and communicating it to the appropriate office staff or executives to support the successful execution of office operations; AND
Independently identifying and performing program assessment and management reviews; AND
Coordinating with internal and external business partners to develop and execute program strategies.
