Assessment 1
Thank you for your interest in a Health Physicist position with the Department of Defense. Your resume and the responses you provide to this assessment questionnaire will be used to determine if you meet the basic eligibility and qualification requirements for this position. Your responses are subject to verification. Please review your responses for accuracy before you submit your application.
1. Select the one statement that best describes the education that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the work of Health Physicist at the GS-13 grade level or equivalent pay band in the Federal Service.

 A. I have a bachelor's degree (or higher degree) in natural science or engineering that included at least 30 semester hours in health physics, engineering, radiological science, chemistry, physics, biology, mathematics, and/or calculus. (Note: You must attach a copy of your transcripts.)
 B. I have a combination of education and experience which includes courses as shown in A above, plus appropriate experience or other education. (Note: You must attach a copy of your transcripts.)
 C. I have a certification as a health physicist by the American Board of Health Physics, plus appropriate experience and other education that provided an understanding of sciences applicable to health physics comparable to that described in paragraph A. (Note: You must attach a copy of your certification and transcripts.)
 D. I do not possess the education described above.
2. Select the one statement that best describes the experience that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the work of Health Physicist at the GS-13 grade level or equivalent pay band in the Federal Service.
 A. I have one year of specialized experience which includes: reviewing Radioactive Material Permits; storing and/or transporting radiation materials; and conducting radiation safety inspections. This definition of specialized experience is typical of work performed at the next lower grade/level position in the federal service (GS-12).
 B. My experience is not reflected in the above statement.
