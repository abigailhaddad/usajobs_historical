 A. I have the ability to perform the most difficult and complex duties of a Painter. I have independently performed painting and surface preparation on historic and modern structures, including visitor centers, maintenance buildings, and outdoor facilities. My experience includes removing old coatings using chemical strippers and mechanical methods, repairing surfaces with fillers and caulking, and applying primers and finish coats using brushes, rollers, and spray equipment. I have matched and blended paints to preserve historical aesthetics and protect surfaces from environmental exposure. I followed all safety protocols for working at heights, handling hazardous materials, and operating equipment. I completed these tasks with minimal supervision, interpreting work orders, selecting appropriate materials, and ensuring high-quality finishes that meet National Park Service standards for preservation and presentation. I am consulted by other painters because of my expertise in applying materials so that surfaces meet match, texture, decorative, sheen, and level requirements. I am considered an expert and work with a high degree of independence.
 B. I have the ability to perform the full range duties as a Painter. I have experience in mixing, matching, tinting and blending coating materials. I am able to apply a smooth finish to surfaces that meet the proper mil thickness requirements. My work is performed using a variety of tools and equipment. My work is assigned in broad general instructions covering the scope of the task assigned. I work on my own initiative under general supervision subject to occasional inspection.
 C. I have the ability to perform common duties as a Painter. I have experience in painting various surfaces ensuring for a smooth surface. I can mix, match, and tint paint. I apply fillers to rough surfaces. I check the requirement’s written instructions, and use a variety of tools. I work under normal supervision of a work leader or journey-level employee who observes tasks in progress and upon completion to make sure they are properly performed.
 D. I have the ability to perform simple duties as a Painter, or I have received training on the use of basic hand tools.
 E. I do not possess the abilities described above.
