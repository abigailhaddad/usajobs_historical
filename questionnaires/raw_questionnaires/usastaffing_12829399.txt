 I have the ability to perform the most difficult and complex of tasks which include; securing floating plant with manila and synthetic lines, cables, wire straps, ratchets, etc.; handling lines in making up and breaking up tows; securing, fastening, hooking, and unhooking floating plant; operating and/or utilizing deck equipment such as small tow-line winch, capstans, winch-counted timber heads, and ratchets in tightening and securing lines and floating plant; deck equipment repair; driving trucks or other vehicles; and general maintenance and housekeeping. I am considered an expert and work with a high degree of independence.
 I have the ability to perform the full range of tasks including; conducting periodic checks and inspections of mooring stabilizing and towing lines, lashing and ratchets, and below dock compartments; replacing frayed lines, tightening loose lashings and lines, replacing broken lines and pumps; conducting channel patrol activities, outboard motor operation, deck equipment repair, driving trucks or other vehicles, general maintenance and housekeeping. I work on my own initiative under general supervision subject to occasional inspection.
 I have the ability to perform common of tasks including; conducting channel patrol activities, outboard motor operation, securing, fastening, hooking, and unhooking floating plant; operating and/or utilizing deck equipment such as small tow-line winch, capstans, winch-counted timber heads, and ratchets in tightening and securing lines and floating plant, deck equipment repair, driving trucks or other vehicles, general maintenance and housekeeping. I work under normal supervision of a work leader or journey-level employee who observes tasks in progress and upon completion to make sure they are properly performed.
 I have the ability to perform simple of tasks including; removing excess oil and grease from machinery; cleaning parts with solvent; performing simple mechanical repairs under direct supervision; performing routine below deck maintenance such as sweeping and cleaning of engine and boiler room; and assisting in general vessel maintenance such as washing and hosing decks as well as chipping and scraping rust.
