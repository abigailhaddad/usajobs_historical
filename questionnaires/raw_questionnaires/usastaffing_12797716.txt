Assessment 1
Thank you for your interest in the Crane Operator position with Department of the Army. Your resume and the responses you provide to this assessment questionnaire will be used to determine if you are among the best qualified for this position. Your responses are subject to verification. Please review your responses for accuracy before you submit your application.
1. From the descriptions below, select the appropriate response based on your level of experience, education, and/or training, that you fully possess and that demonstrates your ability to perform Crane Operator WG-5725-11 duties without more than normal supervision.

 A. I have the ability to perform the most difficult and complex Crane Operator duties including the operation of boom cranes with precision in areas with limited maneuverability, operate at or near the cranes lifting capacity within congested areas, operate cranes at all boom lengths, angles, and make precise load placements with exact movements for accurate and safe positioning. I am considered an expert and work with a high degree of independence.
 B. I have the ability to perform the full range of Crane Operator duties including the operation of boom cranes outdoors with minimal maneuverability or accuracy concerns, operate crane with standard boom lengths and safe load limits, determine the weight of the load to be lifted to ensure lifting capacity is not exceeded, and plan the positioning of the crane for optimal movement and maximum lifting capacity. I work on my own initiative under general supervision subject to occasional inspection.
 C. I have the ability to perform common Crane Operator duties including the operation of boom cranes outdoors with no major maneuverability or accuracy concerns, operate crane with standard boom lengths and safe load limits, and ensure load does not exceed lifting capacity. I work under normal supervision of a work leader or journey-level employee who observes tasks in progress and upon completion to make sure they are properly performed.
 D. I have the ability to perform simple Crane Operator duties to include the operation of boom cranes at various outdoor locations where maneuverability is not a major problem and accuracy is not critical, operation of crane with standard boom lengths and angles, and operation of crane with loads safely within its lifting capacity or I have received training in the operation of boom cranes at various outdoor locations where maneuverability is not a major problem and accuracy is not critical, operation of crane with standard boom lengths and angles, and operation of crane with loads safely within its lifting capacity
