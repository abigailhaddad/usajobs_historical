1. Select the one statement which best describes the highest level of experience you possess and which demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a Pest Controller, WG-5026-09 without more than normal supervision. Note: Your resume must support the response you select.
 A. I am able to perform the complex tasks of a pest controller such as planning, organizing and directing a pest control program; advise management and facility support personnel on aspects of pest management and control, etc. I conduct troubleshooting inspections of facilities and grounds to identify pest infestations of both common and uncommon, and hard-to-find pests, i.e., termites, beetles and moths in stored products, etc. I complete reports and coordinate work with other functional areas. I develop survey and treatment schedules based on the lifecycles, reproduction patterns and sources of infestation. I identify conditions conducive to pest infestations; interpret blueprints and building plans when planning applications within walls, ceiling, or floors of buildings. I properly mix and apply a variety of insecticides, herbicides, rodenticides, fumigants, repellants and fungicides in proper proportions and select the method of application with minimal hazard to desirable species. I identify situations when chemicals and treatment methods have been ineffective and look for new approaches to exterminate the pests. I maintain, calibrate, and use a variety of equipment, cleaning all equipment using approved methods. I inspect pesticide containers for corrosion and leaks. I follow federal and State regulations and rules and observe safety procedures when storing, mixing, applying and disposing of hazardous toxins. I wear and use property safety equipment and clothing. I am considered to be an expert and perform my duties independently. I have provided guidance to lower graded personnel in performing pest control duties.
 B. I am able to perform full range of pest control tasks to include troubleshooting and inspecting assigned areas for pest infestation and reporting on findings. I develop plans for eradication, control and prevention of all common household, structural, nuisance and plant pests. I select, calculate amounts needed and mix the appropriate type, quantity and strength of general and restricted pesticides and baits. I select and use proper equipment for application preventing contamination or damage to textiles, foodstuffs, domestic animals and other wildlife. I follow federal and State regulations and rules and observe safety procedures when storing, mixing, applying and disposing of hazardous toxins. I wear and use proper safety equipment and clothing.
 C. I am able to perform common pest control tasks to include following pre-established plans for eradication and control of common and uncommon pests. I have experience with plant, insect and animal pests. I select, mix and apply general use (over the counter with higher concentrate) chemical pesticides and herbicides using mathematical calculations to determine rate and quantity following product label instructions. I set out traps, pellets, and other types of bait/traps; identify holes, vents, or other structural defects in buildings when pests may be able to enter and notifies supervisor. I select, mix and apply herbicides to kill or prevent growth of noxious plants/weeds along fence lines or outside building, etc. I follow Federal and state regulations regarding safe handling of hazardous toxins. I wear and use appropriate safety clothing and equipment and have had safety training.
 D. I am able to perform simple tasks such setting and putting out various traps and baits, and removing and disposing of animals caught in traps; assisting in selecting, mixing and applying hazardous pesticides; following Federal and state requirements and related safety standards regarding handling of hazardous toxins, but I know little or nothing about pest control work.
