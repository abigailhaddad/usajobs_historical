Assessment 1
Thank you for your interest in a Heavy Mobile Equipment Repairer WG-5803-08 position with the Department of Army. We will evaluate your resume and the responses you provide to this assessment questionnaire to determine if you are considered qualified for this position. Your responses are subject to verification. Please review your responses for accuracy before you submit your application.
1. From the descriptions below, select the appropriate response based on your level of experience, education, and/or training, that you fully possess and that demonstrates your ability to perform Heavy Mobile Equipment Repairer duties without more than normal supervision.

 A. I have the ability to perform the most difficult and complex of maintenance, repairs, troubleshooting, and replacements to components of heavy mobile equipment. I am skilled in components and parts repairs; conducting various operational inspections to insure the vehicles are properly serviced; and operate equipment to check work. I am considered an expert and work with a high degree of independence.
 B. I have the ability to perform the full range of maintenance, repairs, and replacements to component parts of heavy mobile equipment. I am skilled in components and parts repairs; conducting basic operational inspections to insure the vehicles are properly serviced; and operate equipment to check work. I work on my own initiative under general supervision subject to occasional inspection.
 C. I have the ability to perform common maintenance, repairs, and replacements to component parts of heavy mobile equipment. I can perform preventive maintenance checks and services; conducting basic operational inspections to insure the vehicles are properly serviced; and operate equipment to check work. I work on my own initiative under general supervision subject to occasional inspection. I work under close supervision of a work leader or journey-level employee who observes tasks in progress and upon completion to make sure they are properly performed.
 D. I have the ability to perform simple repairs to components of heavy mobile equipment. I have received training on repairs and the makeup and operation of the various individual systems regarding heavy mobile equipment.
 E. I do not possess the abilities described above.
