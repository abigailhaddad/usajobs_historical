Assessment 1
1. From the responses below, select the one response that best describes how you meet the minimum qualification requirements for a YCC Cook WG-7404-08. Read all of the responses before making your selection. MARK ONLY ONE RESPONSE. If you select more than one response or leave this question blank you will be rated ineligible. If you do not meet these minimum qualifications, you will be considered ineligible and will not receive consideration for this position.
 A. I have the knowledge and experience to create nutritional, varied, and enjoyable menus in a timely manner. I have on the job experience preparing meals for large groups of people (50 or more) in a professional setting with a variety of dietary needs. I am comfortable and understand the nature of a professional kitchen and able to make last minute adjustments to volume, and available ingredients. I have extensive knowledge of cooking techniques involved in preparing meats (beef, chicken, fish, pork), sauces, and desserts (mousses, custards, cakes, cookies). I am comfortable cooking for people with dietary needs (vegetarian, vegan, diabetic, etc.) and food allergies. I am trained in proper sanitation and food storage principles. I have experience monitoring inventory and ordering food. I am able to stay calm and professional under stress. I have a good sense of food timing and can consistently produce volume and a variety of complex dishes within the time allowed.
 B. I can prepare meals independently without supervision. I have on the job experience preparing meals for large groups and preparing menus and recipes including several entrees and side dishes per meal. I have good knife skills and a good sense of food preparation timing. I understand culinary techniques such as roasting, braising, sautéing, poaching and baking. I am able to keep a clean and orderly workstation.
 C. I have on the job experience preparing basic meals with multiple menu items without supervision. I can follow recipes and proper cooking procedures. I have experience preparing entrees, salads, and desserts and am comfortable preparing several types of cuisines. I am also able to adjust amounts of common spices or herbs to taste. I can use procedures for sanitary handling and storage of food.
 D. I have the ability to do a wide variety of simple cooking tasks without being told how to do the work (i.e., assisting in grilling chops and poultry, by turning as required and removing when done; heating canned vegetables; and adding salt and pepper seasonings). I have the ability to multiply ingredients of a recipe. I have safely used kitchen equipment. I am able to follow procedures for sanitary handling and storage of food.
 E. I have limited cooking experience. I am familiar with basic techniques such as grilling, frying and baking. I can keep a clean and orderly workplace.
