Select “Yes” or “No” to the following question(s).
1. Do you have the ability to be Lead or Supervise as a Maintenance Mechanic Supervisor. Responsibilities include being Supervisor of the three shops including the carpentry/painting, electrical and maintenance mechanic departments. Incumbent must be able to supervise all the personnel assigned to these units and act as a backup to the other shop supervisor. Incumbent must have some basic knowledge of the technical practices for HVAC, mechanical, boiler plant, pipefitting and plumbing crafts, as well as knowledge of the tools, equipment, and materials as well as safety. The incumbent analyzes workload and support medical center needs to establish deadlines, set priorities, and adjusts work assignments to accomplish assigned tasks. Incumbent will facilitate completion of repairs and projects that involve multi-disciplines across shops within supervisory responsibility. Additional duties including shoveling/clearing snow on a contingency basis as needed to supplement snow crews when snow fall is heavy and/or snow regular snow crew personnel are unavailable and a potential safety hazard from snow and ice is imminent. Responsibilities also include participation in achieving the goals of labor management relations, advises the Maintenance Supervisor and Assistant Chief, FMS of any problems with major critical equipment, overtime needs, or problems with resource acquisition or allocation.
 A. Yes
