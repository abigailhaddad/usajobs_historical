4. GS 08: Experience. One year of experience equivalent to the next lower grade level GS-7. Examples of experience include but are not limited to: Receive ongoing guidance from more experienced staff members and receive general direction by the treating PT. Draw upon a broad base of practical and experiential knowledge in order to assist the PT in reaching pre-determined patient goals. Work independently but still under the general direction of PTs. Observe patients' ability to perform specified tasks in order to provide ongoing assessment of information on patients' physical and functional status. Work with the physical therapy staff to assess progress, modify treatment and set discharge plan. Provide clinical direction for PTA students.
