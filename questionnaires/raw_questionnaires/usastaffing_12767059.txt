THE RESUME OR APPLICATION MATERIALS YOU SUBMIT FOR THIS ANNOUNCEMENT MUST SUPPORT THE ANSWERS YOU CLAIM UNDER THIS AND OTHER QUESTIONS. IF NOT, YOU WILL BE FOUND TO BE INELIGIBLE FOR THIS POSITION OR YOUR SCORE WILL BE LOWERED.
 A. I am able to perform the most difficult and complex maintenance worker (trails) duties. This includes trail construction of major trail bridges, staircases, retaining walls, switchbacks, turnpikes, and water bars without assistance. I have utilized advance rigging techniques using block, tackle, and winch systems. I have used chainsaws to fall hazard trees, remove root balls, and clear fallen trees from trails. I am considered an expert and work with a high degree of independence. I can lift and carry objects weighing more than 50 pounds.
 B. I have the ability to perform the full range of maintenance worker (trails) duties. This includes trail construction and maintenance of minor trail bridges, steps, retaining walls, water bars, and turnpikes without assistance. I have utilized advance rigging techniques using block, tackle, and winch systems. I have used chainsaws to fell trees for bridge stringers, to remove root balls, and clear fallen trees from trails. I work under my own initiative under general supervision subject to occasional inspection. I can lift and carry objects weighing more than 50 pounds.
 C. I have the ability to perform common maintenance worker (trails) duties. This includes trail construction and maintenance of trail signs, turnpikes water bars, step over drains, grade reversals, and trail tread. I have utilized rigging techniques using block, tackle, and winch systems. I have used chainsaws to fell trees and clear fallen trees from trails. I have completed these tasks under normal supervision. I can lift and carry objects weighing more than 50 pounds.
 D. I have the ability to perform simple maintenance worker (trails) duties such as removing roots and rocks, clearing drains, and removing brush. I have used chainsaws to remove fallen trees from trails. I can lift and carry objects weighing more than 50 pounds.
 E. I do not possess the abilities described above.
