Assessment 1
1. Select the one statement that best describes the Minimum Requirements that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a GS-0301-11 or higher.
 A. I HAVE experience, education, or training 1) which provided me a general knowledge of principles of organization, management, and administration and 2) using computer and automation systems.
 B. I DO NOT HAVE the experience, education, or training listed above.
2. Select the one statement that best describes the Specialized Experience and/or education that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a GS-0301-11 or higher.
 A. I HAVE at least 36 months experience, education or training in 1) planning, organizing, coordinating, implementing, evaluating, and providing technical guidance to units and major commands in the state, 2) developing and recommending plans, procedures and policies, 3) analyzing the management of the state ARNG Force Integration Program, 4) coordinating the development and update of Table of Distribution and Allowance (TDAs) and Modified Table of Organization and Equipment (MTOE).
 B. I HAVE experience and/or education (from an accredited university) to meet the overall specialized experience.
 C. I DO NOT have at least 36 months of the experience, education or training listed above.
