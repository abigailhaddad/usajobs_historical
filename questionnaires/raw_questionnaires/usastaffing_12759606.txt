Assessment 1
Once the application process is complete, a review of your resume and supporting documentation will be completed. If, after reviewing your resume and supporting documentation, it is determined that you have misrepresented your qualifications and/or experience, a revised assessment will be rendered.
1. Select the one statement that best describes the Minimum Requirements that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a WG-8602-10 Aircraft Engine Mechanic.
 A. I HAVE experience or training 1) that has provided me the ability to perform assembly and disassembly operations and make prescribed modifications to aircraft engines, engine assemblies, and accessories in accordance with technical specifications, procedures and requirements, and 2) using hand and power tools common to the trade.
 B. I DO NOT HAVE above listed experience or training.
2. Select the one statement that best describes the Specialized Experience and/or education that you possess that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a WG-8602-10 Aircraft Engine Mechanic.
 A. I HAVE at least 18 months experience or training 1) performing standard, routine assembly and disassembly operations and making prescribed modifications to aircraft engines, engine assemblies, and accessories, 2) performing maintenance overhaul, troubleshooting, testing, and repair of aircraft engines, engine components, and power trains for fixed and/or rotary wing aircraft, and 3) determining maintenance needed from work orders, inspection reports, historical record, and by own diagnosis.
 B. I DO NOT have at least 18 months of the experience, education or training listed above.
