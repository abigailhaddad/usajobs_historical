1.
I have the ability to Lead the work of Food Service Worker. This would include communicating instructions and information to staff regarding preparing a variety of foods, determining which food items to serve for common, specialized or modified diets; communicating with supervisors about problems and events that occurred; providing on-the-job training; reviewing daily work assignments and making adjustments when needed. I perform food service work to include reviewing trays before delivery ensuring correct food temperature, selections and portions; assisting cooks with food preparation; preparing special feeding and nourishment items according to label or oral instructions; serving meals and drinks, clean and sanitizing kitchen equipment; cleaning tables and washing dishes. I am capable of following instructions and can follow proper food handling guidelines; I have the ability to make arithmetical computations to increase, decrease or convert metric to standard measurements.
