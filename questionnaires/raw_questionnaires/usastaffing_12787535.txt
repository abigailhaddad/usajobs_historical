Assessment 1
This section will be used to determine if you possess the experience needed to qualify for a Powered Support Systems Mechanic , WG-5378-10 positions. Failure to respond to this question will result in an ineligible rating.
1. Select the one statement which best describes the highest level of experience you possess and which demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of a Powered Support Systems Mechanic , WG-5378-10 without more than normal supervision. Note: Your resume must support the response you select.
 A. I am able to perform complex Powered Support Equipment Repairer tasks including installing, inspecting, and performing modifications and repairs to electrical generators both real property and mobile. Troubleshoot electrical power support systems such as electrical generating plants and automatic transfer switches and various designs and manufacture. Maintain, inspect, repairs, diagnoses, and modifies aircraft arresting systems. Utilizes safety practices and procedures following established safety rules and regulations and maintains a safe and clean work environment.
 B. I am able to perform a full range of Powered Support Equipment Repairer tasks including installing, inspecting, and performing modifications and repairs to electrical generators both real property and mobile. Troubleshoot electrical power support systems such as electrical generating plants and automatic transfer switches and various designs and manufacture. Maintain, inspect, repairs, diagnoses, and modifies aircraft arresting systems. Utilizes safety practices and procedures following established safety rules and regulations and maintains a safe and clean work environment on my own initiative under limited supervision subject to occasional inspection.
 C. I am able to perform common Powered Support Equipment Repairer tasks including installing, inspecting, and performing modifications and repairs to electrical generators both real property and mobile. Maintain, inspect, repairs, diagnoses, and modifies aircraft arresting systems. Utilizes safety practices and procedures following established safety rules and regulations and maintains a safe and clean work environment. I work under the close supervision of a journeyman Equipment Repairer who observes tasks in progress and upon completion to make sure they are properly performed.
 D. I am able to perform simple Powered Support Equipment Repairer tasks including maintaining records and documenting actions. Performing clean-up duties such as cleaning equipment, sweeping, moping, straightening, and lining up tools and other property in the assigned area.
 E. I have no experience or training as an Powered Support Equipment Repairer
