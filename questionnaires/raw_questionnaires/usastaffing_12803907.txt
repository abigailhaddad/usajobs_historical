Assessment 1
Thank you for your interest in the Department of the Navy Nonappropriated Funds (NAF) Employment. This section will be used to determine if you possess the specialized experience, education, and/or training to qualify for the NAF Community Recreation Director position.  Failure to meet the basic requirement(s) and/or specialized experience; failure to respond to the question(s); and/or failure to provide required documentation will result in an ineligible rating.  Please ensure your resume fully supports the response you choose for each of the questions below and required documentation uploaded at the time of submission of application/resume packet for this position.
1. Choose the statement that best describes your education and/or experience related to this position.  (Resume MUST reflect experience and MUST provide official transcripts)
 A. 4-year undergraduate degree in Recreation. (MUST provide official transcripts)
 B. 3 years of full time comparable work experience that illustrates a progression in management experience. (Resume MUST reflect experience)
 C. At least 1 year of full time work experience in a directly related field at the NF-03/GS-07/08 level is required. (Resume MUST reflect experience and MUST upload personnel action/SF50 equivalent)
 D. None of the statements apply to me.
2. Choose the statement that best applies to you. (Resume MUST reflect experience)
 A. Currently holds a Community Recreation Director or supervisory/managerial position managing 2 or more programs related to community recreation (e.g., Outdoor Recreation, ITT, Special Events, Liberty Programs, Auto Hobby, Arts & Crafts, Library, etc.) within CNIC. (Resume must reflect experience)
 B. Currently holds a Community Recreation Director or supervisory/managerial position managing 2 or more programs related to community recreation (e.g., Outdoor Recreation, ITT, Special Events, Liberty Programs, Auto Hobby, Arts & Crafts, Library, etc.) within DoD. (Resume must reflect experience)
 C. Has held a Community Recreation Director or supervisory/managerial position managing 2 or more programs related to community recreation (e.g., Outdoor Recreation, ITT, Special Events, Liberty Programs, Auto Hobby, Arts & Crafts, Library, etc.) within the last 3 years. (Resume must reflect experience)
 D. None of the statements apply to me.

 B. Has 3+ years of full time work experience working in a supervisory/managerial position in programs related to community recreation (e.g., Outdoor Recreation, ITT, Special Events, Liberty Programs, Auto Hobby, Arts & Crafts, Library, etc.). (Resume must reflect experience)
