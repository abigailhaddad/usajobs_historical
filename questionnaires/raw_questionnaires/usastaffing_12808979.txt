1.
BASIC REQUIREMENTS: MUST posses one of the following,
Degree in Architecture:
A bachelor's or higher degree in architecture that includes at least 60 semester hours in architecture or related disciplines, with 30 semester hours in architectural design and 6 semester hours in each of the following: structural technology,properties of materials/methods of construction, and environmental control systems. OR a bachelor's or higher degree in architectural engineering (or another closely related field) may be accepted if the completed coursework provided knowledge, skills, and abilities substantially equivalent to those specified above for an architecture degree.
  - OR - 
Combination of education and experience:
College-level education, training, and/or technical experience that together provided: A thorough knowledge of the arts and sciences underlying professional architecture, and a good understanding (theoretical and practical) of architectural principles, methods, and techniques and their applications to design and construction.
If lacking a degree in architecture must have had l year of experience in an architect's office or in architectural work for each year short of graduation from a program of study in architecture. In the absence of college courses, 5 years of such experience is required. This experience must have demonstrated a thorough knowledge of the fundamental principles and theories of professional architecture.
