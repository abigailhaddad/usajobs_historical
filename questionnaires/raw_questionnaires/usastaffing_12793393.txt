GRADE DETERMINATIONS. In addition to the basic requirements for this position, the following criteria must be met when determining the grade of candidates.
3. GS-7 Health Technician (Telehealth Clinical) (Advanced Level)
Specialized Experience: At least 1 year of experience equivalent to the next lower grade (GS-6) level that demonstrates the technical competencies described at this level. Experience for this position includes that which provides a working knowledge and ability to screen patients for Telehealth modality and refer patients that do not meet established criteria to appropriate care; capture and transmission of clinical images and/or data, obtains consent as necessary, generates consults according to established procedures for each telehealth specialty, transmits images or clinical data to the electronic medical record, provides patient education regarding general anatomy, disease processes and prevention as appropriate, and takes appropriate action based on the providers' findings and recommendations; provides hands on assistance and reports clinical observations under the direction of a licensed independent Practitioner or clinical specialist; work out process issues, equipment needs, problems, data collection and any other logistical issues; conducts routine inventories and consistently tracking location/status of Telehealth technology located at assigned site; troubleshoots, repairs, and coordination of replacement equipment as necessary.
