 A. I have the ability to perform the most complex and difficult tasks as a work leader for 5 or more employees in the performance of trail rehabilitation. I have performed and led crews in construction and maintenance of major trail bridges, rock/log staircases, multi-tier retaining walls, switchbacks, turnpikes, water bars, and grade reversals without assistance. I have utilized advanced rigging techniques using block, tackle and winch systems to remove trail hazards as well as in projects such as bridge construction and staircase or wall construction. I have used chainsaws to fall hazard trees, remove large root balls, and clear fallen trees from trails. I am considered an expert and work with a high degree of independence. I can lift and carry objects weighing more than 50 pounds.
 B. I have the ability to perform the full range of activities as a work leader providing leadership and guidance for 3 or more employees performing trail work. I have led a crew in trail construction and maintenance of minor trail bridges, rock/log steps, retaining walls, water bars, and turnpikes without assistance. I have utilized advanced rigging techniques using block, tackle and winch systems to remove trail hazards as well as in projects such as bridge construction and stair or wall construction. I have used chainsaws to fall trees for bridge stringers, remove small root balls, and clear fallen trees from trails. I work on my own initiative under general supervision subject to occasional inspection. I can lift and carry objects weighing more than 50 pounds.
 C. I have the ability to perform common tasks associated with trail work and have some experience as a work leader on a trail crew or group responsible for carrying out work on trails. This includes trail construction and maintenance of trail signs, turnpikes, water bars, step over drains, grade reversals, and trail tread. I have utilized rigging techniques using block, tackle and winch systems to remove trail hazards as well as in small scale trail projects. I have used chainsaws to fall trees for project materials and clear fallen trees from trails. I have completed these tasks under normal supervision. I can lift and carry objects weighing more than 50 pounds.
 D. I have the ability to perform simple tasks associated with trail work and have limited experience as a work leader. I have performed tasks such as rebenching tread, removing roots and rocks, cleaning drains, and removing brush. I have used chainsaws to remove fallen trees from trails. I can lift and carry objects weighing more than 50 pounds.
 E. I have no experience supervising or leading a trails crew as described above
