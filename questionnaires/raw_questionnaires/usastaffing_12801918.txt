1. Read all of the statements below, and then select the response based on your highest level of experience and/or training that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of the position.
 A. I am able to perform complex Aircraft Electrician tasks such as troubleshooting, modifying, repairing, and performing final functional and operational tests of complex electrical systems, components, and accessories in fixed winged aircraft. Troubleshooting, modifying, repairing, and testing complex electrical systems, components, and accessories with intricate wiring systems under actual or ground power such as flight controls, landing gear, antiskid, ignition, fire warning, power distribution, engine driven generators, and bleed-air and pressurization systems. Installing non-conventional electric and related equipment by routing and connecting wiring to new or existing electrical or electronic systems, components, or equipment. Assisting engineering personnel in developing modifications and changes on electrical, electronic, instrument, and other integrated electrical systems. with a high degree of independence.
 B. I am able to perform a full range of Aircraft Electrician tasks such as setting up and operating computerized multiple circuit analyzing equipment to run established and new diagnostic programs in various test modes to test aircraft electrical circuitry and interconnecting cabling of the entire aircraft or individual systems such as primary and secondary flight controls, power generating systems, bleed air and pressurization, and landing gear on my own initiative under limited supervision subject to occasional inspection.
 C. I am able to perform common Aircraft Electrician tasks such as using of a variety of tools and equipment such as hand and electrical test units, e.g., meters, bridges, meggers, harness testers, locally manufactured "breakout boxes", signal generators, oscilloscopes, timing devices, calibrators, multiple function analyzers, and computerized multiple circuit analyzing equipment. Providing technical advice/assistance regarding, safety practices and procedures following established safety rules and regulations and maintains a safe and clean work environment. I work under the close supervision of a journeyman electrician who observes tasks in progress and upon completion to make sure they are properly performed.
