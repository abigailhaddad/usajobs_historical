 A. I have experience operating multiple types of heavy engineering equipment inclusive of but not limited to: gasoline or diesel-powered heavy equipment, five and fifteen cubic-yard dump trucks, road graders, and front-end loaders. I have operated this equipment to move and load material such as dirt, snow, and other material. I can operate vehicles with auxiliary attachments (e.g., snow plow blade, rotary snow blower, mower, sweeper, sanding units) to perform duties. I can operate trucks and equipment with multiple operating functions like front loader/compactor trash trucks and snow removal equipment. I am able to operate various types of equipment and trucks on congested roadways, in inclement weather and on steep and narrow roadways. I often work independently with little to no supervision.
 B. I have experience operating one or more types of the heavy engineering equipment listed in A above. I have operated this equipment to move and load material. I can operate vehicles with auxiliary attachments to perform duties. I can operate trucks and equipment with multiple operating functions like front loader/compactor trash trucks and snow removal equipment under normal supervision. I am able to operate various types of equipment and trucks on congested roadways, in inclement weather and on steep and narrow roadways. I've worked with only general managerial or technical assistance in the accomplishment of duties and assignments.
 C. I have experience operating one or more types of the heavy engineering equipment listed in A above. I have operated this equipment to move and load material. I can operate vehicles with auxiliary attachments to perform duties. I have little experience in snow removal and trash truck operation. I am able to operate various types of equipment and trucks on congested roadways, in inclement weather and on steep and narrow roadways. I am able to perform the tasks as described in "A" under close supervision of a worker leader or journey person who makes sure they are properly performed.
 D. I have no experience in operating heavy engineering equipment as described above, but I'm willing to learn.
