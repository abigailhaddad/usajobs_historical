1. From the descriptions below, select the appropriate response based on your level of experience, education, and/or training, that you fully possess and that demonstrates your ability to perform Lock and Dam Equipment Repairer Supervisor duties without more than normal supervision.
 A. I have the ability to perform the most difficult and complex Lock and Dam Equipment Repairer Supervisor duties. I am skilled in 1) supervising tasks as a journeyman level Lock and Dam Equipment Repairer Supervisor; 2) leading site operating and normal maintenance activities involving lock and dam operation and associated buildings, grounds, and equipment operation and maintenance; 3) planning the use of subordinate workers, equipment, facilities and materials; 4) assigning work to individual employees involved in lock and dam operating and site maintenance activities; and 5) overseeing and performing the full range of repairs at lock and dam sites. I am considered an expert and work with a high degree of independence.
 B. I have the ability to perform the full range of Lock and Dam Equipment Repairer Supervisor duties. I am skilled in 1) leading site operating and normal maintenance activities involving lock and dam operation and associated buildings, grounds, and equipment operation and maintenance; 2) planning the use of subordinate workers, equipment, facilities and materials; 3) assigning work to individual employees involved in lock and dam operating and site maintenance activities; and 4) performing the full range of repairs at lock and dam sites. I work on my own initiative under general supervision subject to occasional inspection.
 C. I have the ability to perform common Lock and Dam Equipment Repairer Supervisor duties. I am skilled in 1) planning the use of subordinate workers, equipment, facilities and materials; 2) assigning work to individual employees involved in lock and dam operating and site maintenance activities; and 3) performing the full range of repairs at lock and dam sites. I work under normal supervision of a work leader or journey-level employee who observes tasks in progress and upon completion to make sure they are properly performed.
 D. I have the ability to perform simple Lock and Dam Equipment Repairer Supervisor duties. I can perform general repairs at lock and dam sites.
 E. I do not possess the abilities described above.
