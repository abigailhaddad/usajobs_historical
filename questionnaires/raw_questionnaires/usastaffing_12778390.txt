1. Do you have the ability to do the work of a Electrical Supervisor with the ability to supervise in performing the full range of this position? Related experience includes plans, assigns, accomplishes and coordinates maintenance and repair work request of the Electrical shop by establishing deadlines and priorities either set by him/herself or by higher level supervisors. He/she furnishes instructions, assigns and directs subordinates and orders parts, materials and equipment. Planning is performed on a day to day, project by project, week to week, month to month basis. Preventative maintenance is expected to be planed a minimum of two weeks in advanced to allow the parts to come in before service. The incumbent must have an extensive knowledge of the electrical distribution systems ranging from 15KV, 5KV, 480v, 277v, 208v, 120v, three-phase and single phase distribution, emergency and non-emergency generators, system control and data acquisition, DDC computerized control, UPS, low voltage (AC and DC), and fire alarms. He/she must have thorough knowledge of the National Electric Code and basic knowledge of construction procedures and how to apply this knowledge in the alteration of buildings and installation of electrical systems is expected.
