Ability to Do the Work of a Tractor Operator without More than Normal Supervision (Screen out)
1. From the descriptions below, select the one response that best describes your highest level of experience and/training performing tractor operator duties. Your claim must be supported in your resume.

 A. I am able to perform and/or have led the full range of the most difficult and complex tractor operator duties with a high degree independence and initiative. The work included operation of field-type tractors over uneven ground such as open fields, banks, ditches, lawns, hills and roadsides; connecting various attachments such as blades, harrows, trailers, and brush hogs, and manipulate controls as required; apply a good knowledge of controls for starting, stopping, and maneuvering while judging turn angles of tractor and towed vehicles/equipment, speed, clearances, cargo weight, etc; interpret and follow oral and written instructions; perform routine operator maintenance to include lubricating the equipment and checking and replenishing fluids to proper levels; frequently lifting and carrying parts and equipment weighing up to 50 pounds, and frequently having to stoop, bend, climb, and work in cramped positions; following prescribed safety practices and using required/appropriate safety equipment.
 B. I am able to perform tractor operation under general supervision subject to occasional inspection. Work experience included operation of field-type tractors over uneven ground such as open fields, banks, ditches, lawns, hills and roadsides; connecting various attachments such as blades, harrows, trailers, and brush hogs, and manipulate controls as required; apply a good knowledge of controls for starting, stopping, and maneuvering while judging turn angles of tractor and towed vehicles/equipment, speed, clearances, cargo weight, etc; perform routine operator maintenance to include lubricating the equipment and checking and replenishing fluids to proper levels.
 C. I am able to perform tractor operation under close supervision subject to frequent inspection. Work experience included operation of field/farm type tractors such as a John Deere 5100, 100 HP or size equivalent, and a John Deere 6200 95 HP or size equivalent, over uneven terrain such as open fields, ditches, banks, lawns, hills, steep backcountry roads, paved roads, sheer drop offs, muddy conditions, etc. Hooks up and operates various attachments and auxiliary equipment such as backhoes, boom mowers, rear flail mowers, brush hogs, wood chippers, front end loaders, post hole diggers, street sweepers, and beach cleaners.
 D. I have no on-the-job training or experience performing the tasks listed in "A" above, but I am willing to learn.
