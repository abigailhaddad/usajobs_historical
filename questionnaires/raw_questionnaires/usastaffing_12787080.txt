 A. I have the ability to perform the most difficult and complex duties of a Painting Worker. I independently prepare surfaces by cleaning, sanding, and masking to ensure proper adhesion of coatings. I apply paints, varnishes, and other finishes using brushes, rollers, and spray guns. I follow detailed instructions for mixing and applying coating materials ensuring surfaces are evenly coated, protected, and free of defects such as drips, runs, or bare spots using standard methods and materials. I use basic tools and equipment safely and maintain them in good working conditions. I am able to read and interpret written instructions and specifications. I plan my work sequences and track labor hours, tools, and materials costs. I select proper tools and otherwise carry out assignments through to completion. My work is reviewed in terms of the overall results achieved. I am consulted by other painters because of my expertise in applying materials so that surfaces meet match, texture, decorative, sheen, and level requirements. I am considered an expert and work with a high degree of independence.
 B. I have the ability to perform the full range duties as a Painting Worker. I have experience preparing surfaces by cleaning, sanding, and masking to ensure proper adhesion of coatings. I am able to apply paints, varnishes, and other finishes using brushes, rollers, and spray guns. My work is performed using a variety of tools and equipment. My work is assigned in broad general instructions covering the scope of the task assigned. I work on my own initiative under general supervision subject to occasional inspection.

I work under normal supervision of a work leader or journey-level employee who observes tasks in progress and upon completion to make sure they are properly performed.
