Assessment 1
Thank you for your interest in the Department of the Navy Nonappropriated Funds Employment. This section will be used to determine if you possess the education and/or experience to qualify for the Construction Contract Specialist position. Failure to respond to the question(s) will result in an ineligible rating. Please ensure that your resume and/or transcript fully support the response you choose for each of the questions below.
1. Which of the following do you possess?
 A. A 4-year course of study leading to a bachelor’s degree with a major in any field.
 B. At least 24 semester hours in any combination of the following fields: accounting, business, finance, law, contracts, purchasing, economics, industrial management, marketing, quantitative methods, or organization and management.
 C. I do not possess any of the above.
Select “Yes” or “No” to the following question(s).
2. Do you have four or more years of specialized experience implementing policies and practices of general and/or construction contracting?
 A. Yes
 B. No
3. Which statement best describes your experience with Acquisition Procedures and Regulations?
 A. My current position requires knowledge and application of Federal Acquisition Regulations, NAF Construction Regulations, and/or other governmental regulations relating to NAF and/or APF construction contracting.
 B. My previous position (held within the last 5 years) required knowledge and application of Federal Acquisition Regulations, NAF Construction Regulations and/or other governmental regulations relating to NAF and/or APF construction contracting.
 C. My current and/or previous position (held within the last 5 years) required experience with and knowledge of other governmental (non-Federal) construction contracting procedures and regulations.
 D. My current and/or previous position (held more than 5 years ago) required knowledge and application of governmental and/or commercial contracting regulations, not related to construction.
 E. I do not have any experience with the above.
4. Which statement best describes your experience in a construction contracting position?
 A. I currently work in a NAF or APF Federal government construction contracting position.
 B. I have past (held within the last three years) work experience in a NAF or APF Federal government construction contracting position.
 C. I have past (held more than 3 years ago) work experience in an NAF or APF Federal government construction contracting position.
 D. I have work experience in a construction contracting position other than NAF or APF.
 E. I do not have construction contracting experience.
5. How many years of experience experience do you have evaluating, certifying, and auditing contract proposals, awards and disbursements, or acquisition reviews?
 A. I have 5+ years of experience with the above.
 B. I have 4 to 5 years of experience with the above.
 C. I have 3 to 4 years of experience with the above.
 D. I have less than 3 years of experience with the above
