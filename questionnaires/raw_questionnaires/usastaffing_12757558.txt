Thank you for your interest in a career as a Patient Representative with the Veterans Health Administration. The following will determine that you meet the minimum qualifications for this position. Please refer to the qualifications section of the announcement for more information on minimum qualifications. Please select the appropriate answer to each of the following questions based on your current level of education and/or experience that demonstrates your ability to perform the duties of this position. When answering the questionnaire, remember your experience and education are subject to verification by investigation.

Time-in-Grade Requirements: This is an internal Department of Veterans Affairs announcement and applicants who are current VA employees and have held a GS grade any time in the past 52 weeks must also meet time-in-grade requirements. For this vacancy, applicants must have held grade GS-09 for a year to be eligible for the GS-11. This grade level must have been held in Federal service but may have been in any occupation.

You Must Submit Relevant SF-50s as Described in the Announcement or You Will be Found Ineligible

The following questions will determine your basic eligibility for the Patient Representative position to which you are applying.
1. Time-In-Grade GS-11: Please select the response that most accurately states your eligibility for the time-in-grade requirement to be considered for a GS-11 position.
 A. I have served one year (52 weeks), or more, at the GS-09 grade level or higher
 B. I currently hold a GS-11 position
 C. I do not meet the time-in-grade requirement for this grade level and understand that I am not eligible to be considered for this position
2. Specialized Experience Requirement GS-11: Carefully read the following descriptions below. Select the one response below that best describes your specialized experience and/or education for this position. (Please ensure your resume/application supports the response that you select.)
 A. I possess one (1) full year of specialized experience equivalent to the GS-9 level in the Federal Service that has equipped me with the particular knowledge, skills and abilities to perform successfully the duties of the position and that is typically in or related to the work of this position. Qualifying experience includes, but is not limited to: managing a facility VCCR process, including complaint resolution, data capture and analysis of issues/complaints to make system improvements; serve as liaison between the Facility, patients, staff, and the community, ensuring Patient Rights and Responsibilities, advocacy, and services are available; directly supporting facility Executive Leadership responsible for planning, organization, development, direction and evaluation of VA Medical Facility administration; maintaining a strong and consistent focus on Complaint Resolution which must always be accompanied by attempts at Service Recovery.
 B. I do not possess the specialized experience listed above
