 A. I have successfully completed a Bachelor's degree in computer science or bachelor's degree with 30 semester hours in a combination of mathematics, statistics, and computer science. I have at least one year of specialized experience which is equivalent to Federal experience at the next lower broadband NH-03, equivalent to the next to lower grade GS-13 or equivalent in the Federal Service).
