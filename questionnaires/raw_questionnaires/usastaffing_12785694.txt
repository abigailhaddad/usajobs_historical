Eligibilities
1. Do you claim Veterans’ Preference?
 A. No, I do not claim Veterans' Preference
 B. 0-point Sole Survivorship Preference (SSP)
 C. 5-point preference based on active duty in the U.S. Armed Forces (TP)
 D. 10-point preference for non-compensable disability or Purple Heart (XP)
 E. 10-point preference based on a compensable service connected disability of at least 10% but less than 30% (CP)
 F. 10 point preference based on widow/widower or parent of a deceased veteran, or spouse or parent of a disabled veteran (XP)
 G. 10-point preference based on a compensable service connected disability of 30% or more (CPS)
Assessment 1
Thank you for your interest in the Department of the Navy Non-appropriated Funds Employment. This section will be used to determine if you possess the education and/or experience to qualify for a Recreation Aid position. Failure to respond to the question(s) will result in an ineligible rating.
Select “Yes” or “No” to the following question(s).
1. Do you currently have Command Sponsorship within the local commuting area for this position?

If YES, please confirm ONE of the following:

1. You have Command Sponsorship through an Active-Duty Service Member as a sponsor. You will need to submit your sponsor's official PCS Orders and Family (Dependent) Entry of Approval with your name listed as an approved dependent.

2. You have Command Sponsorship through a Federal Civilian Stateside Hire as a sponsor. You will need to submit your sponsor's Letter of Employment with your name listed as an approved dependent.

Note: You will need to attach and upload your proof of Sponsorship Status to be considered for the position.
