Thank you for your interest in the Dental Assistant position with the Department of Veterans Affairs. Select the response that best describes your experience, education or combination of experience and education.
 A. Experience: Six months experience that demonstrates the applicant’s ability to perform the work, or provides familiarity with the work.

 C. Experience/Education Combination: Equivalent combinations of experience and education are qualifying.
 D. I do not meet the basic experience, education or combination of experience and education requirement.

Successful completion of a radiation health and safety program or examination approved by a state board of dentistry or the Department of Defense (DoD).

3. Grade Determination (GS-06)
Do you possess at least one (1) year of experience equivalent to the next lower grade (GS-5) level that demonstrates the core competencies described at that level?

Examples of experience includes:
Respond to basic medical emergencies;
Measure and records vital signs;
Provide pre- and post-operative instructions;
Maintain a field of operation during dental procedures through the use of retraction,
Suction, irrigation, drying, placing and removing cotton rolls;
Using the concepts of four-handed dentistry,
Assist with basic restorative procedures;
Mix basic dental materials.
 A. Yes
 B. No
4. GS-6 (Full Performance Level) Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities
In addition, the candidate must demonstrate the following Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities (KSAs) 
Ability to identify normal oral anatomy.
Advanced knowledge of instruments, materials, and standardized dental procedures used in all phases of restorative, surgical, endodontic and periodontal care and procedures.
Ability to monitor and perform basic interpretation of vital signs.
Ability to independently provide procedure-specific patient education and appropriate referral of patient concerns to treating dentist.
Ability to capture standard and special dental images of good diagnostic quality in traditional or digital formats.
Ability to perform maintenance on dental equipment used for routine and specialty dentistry.
Ability to enter and retrieve data utilizing electronic dental records.
Ability to perform dental assistant-appropriate laboratory procedures.
