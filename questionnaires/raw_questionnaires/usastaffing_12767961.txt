 A. I have the ability to perform difficult and complex maintenance mechanic tasks to include repair work and operation as electrician, air conditioning mechanic, heating equipment mechanic, plumber, and solar heating system mechanic. I am skilled in 1) Recognize the cause of faulty equipment, and makes repairs to heating and air conditioning equipment, domestic/commercial refrigeration equipment, ventilation equipment, and evaporative cooling equipment; 2) Working on systems such as fired high pressure boilers that maintain prescribed boiler temperatures and pressures; 3) Utilize blueprints to determine any modifications needed in the plumbing system (e.g., openings, slant, level of water, gas lines). Plan and lays out the installation and modification of various systems and equipment such as the routing, openings, slant, and level of gas and water lines; and the location and arrangement of water closets, sinks, and fire sprinkler equipment.; 4) Determine necessary settings and to adjust controls of generating equipment for desired operating conditions and synchronization of equipment with the system.; and 5) Replacing and removing fixtures such as tightening connections, wrapping exposed wiring and soldering loose wire leads to contact points. I am considered an expert and work with a high degree of independence.
 B. I have the ability to perform the full range of maintenance mechanic tasks to include repair work and operation as electrician, air conditioning mechanic, heating equipment mechanic, plumber, and solar heating system mechanic. I am skilled in 1) Servicing power sources, tightening connections, making splices, insulating exposed wires, and cleaning/lubricating moving parts.; 2) Repairing and replacing water regulators, boosters, generators, oil and air supplies and controls, alarms, safety devices, etc.; 3) Installing and modifying potable water disinfectant systems including working with chemicals, analyzers and other components such as digital mixing valves, circulation pumps and other anti-scald devices.; 4) Interpreting readings from charts and meters to determine whether to increase or lower fire in boiler, whether to start or stop boiler feed pumps, and to determine how long these are to operate. I work on my own initiative under general supervision subject to occasional inspection.
 C. I have the ability to perform common Maintenance Mechanic tasks to include repair work and operation as electrician, air conditioning mechanic, heating equipment mechanic, plumber, and solar heating system mechanic. I am skilled in 1) Operating hand and power tools; Uses electrical tools, tools of the trade such tapes, rules, hacksaws, and power pipe threaders, cutters, packing and caulking irons and pipe wrenches.; 2) Removing and replacing fixtures and controls to make repairs such as tightening connections, soldering loose wire threads, and skills to rearrange old or install new outlets, test equipment.; 3) Performing preventive maintenance such as greasing/oiling motors, replacing belts and filters, setting controls, regulators, switches, and starters, replacing parts, repairing refrigerant leaks, and replacing refrigerant. I work under close supervision of a work leader or journey-level employee who observes tasks in progress and upon completion to make sure they are properly performed.
 D. I have the ability to perform simple repair or maintenance work or I have received training on basic repair or maintenance work.
 E. I do not possess the abilities described above.
