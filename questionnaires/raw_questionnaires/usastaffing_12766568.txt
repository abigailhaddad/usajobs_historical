Experience. Completion of one year of progressive experience and/or experience equivalent to the next lower grade level of GS-7 directly related to the position being filled.

For GS-7 Some duties include, but are not limited to: 
The HTA at this level works under supervision and direction of a licensed audiologist. Assignments associated with this position include, but are not limited to: completing adjustments to hearing aid settings as outlined in the audiologist's plan of care, preparing patients for advanced diagnostic procedures such as evoked potentials and collaborating with audiology team to develop training programs for patients and staff.
