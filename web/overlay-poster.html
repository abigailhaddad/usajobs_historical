Postings vs accessions

Each panel overlays USAJobs postings (green bars, intent to hire) and OPM EHRI accessions (orange line, actual hires) for one cabinet department. Both share the same x-axis but use different y-axes because postings and hires are different magnitudes — postings on the left, accessions on the right.

The dashed red line marks the federal hiring freeze (executive order signed Jan 20, 2025). Postings collapsed almost immediately for most departments; accessions follow with a few months of lag because pipelines that started in Dec / Jan continued to close out.
