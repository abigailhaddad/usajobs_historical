Federal workforce flow

Each panel shows monthly accessions (people hired in, green bars going up) and separations (people leaving, red bars going down) for one cabinet department. The dashed red line marks the federal hiring freeze (executive order signed Jan 20, 2025).

If the green bar is taller than the red bar, the workforce is growing that month; if red is taller, it's shrinking. Hover any month to see accessions, separations, and net change.
