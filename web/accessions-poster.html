Federal accessions by department

Monthly new federal hires (accessions) from January 2018 through March 2026, one panel per cabinet department, sorted by recovery (least to most). The dashed red line marks the federal hiring freeze (executive order signed Jan 20, 2025). Hover any bar to see the exact month and count.

What this is: "Accessions" are people who were actually hired into the executive-branch federal workforce, from the OPM EHRI dataset (via Impact Project's HuggingFace mirror). Includes new hires, conversions, and transfers in. Excludes the Postal Service and intelligence community. Data lags by 1–2 months from the most recent USAJobs postings.
